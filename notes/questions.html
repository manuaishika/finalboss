questions i ask myself every quarter or so

a quick gut-check i revisit to stay aligned with what actually matters. got really bored of the pinterest "where do you see yourself in 5 years"fluff.

  • when have i felt most like myself in the last few months or years?
  • what do i do even when no one is watching or praising me?
  • what efforts are truly satisfying and not draining for me?
  • what do i secretly admire in others that i wish i embodied?
  • if no one could see my achievements, what would i still want to achieve?
  • which dreams do i talk about more than i work for?
